Our Process

What goes into a digital marketing campaign?

01 - Research.

USER BASED RESEARCH

Identifying target keywords and auditing your current site and rankings is not enough. We work with you to truly understand the user intent behind search terms. Throw in thorough industry and competitor research. Layer in careful analysis of the SERPs, and we’ll build a seriously impressive results-driven SEO strategy.

02 - Onsite.

ONSITE OPTIMISATION

Content and link building steal the limelight, but onsite optimisation is the foundation of every campaign. Our onsite optimisation process is thorough. We combine user flow, conversion rate optimisation and technical SEO to set solid foundations. We leave no stone unturned. From optimising metadata to encourage high click through rates on SERPs. We assess your robots.txt file and local SEO, as well as mobile responsive design that satisfies Google’s mobile first index, and much more!

03 - Links.

LINK BUILDING

Possibly the most infamous aspect of SEO. Our link building strategies are all above board, complying with Penguin 4.0 and based upon genuine exposure for the client. No linkspam here – you can view our link building as another form of digital PR.

04 - Analytics.

DATA ANALYSIS & ITERATIVE IMPROVEMENT

Data doesn't have to be scary. In fact, for SEO it’s essential for iterative and agile improvement. That’s why we take steps to implement detailed tracking via Google Analytics, Search Console and SEMrush from the very beginning. We analyse user engagement & flow. We review search data, keyword rankings and traffic volume. We’ll assess goal conversions and CRO to continually improve results.

05 - Reporting.

SEO REPORTING

As an SEO agency we pride ourselves on the level of detail we incorporate into our bespoke, easy to understand reports. It’s just as important that our clients understand the why as well as the what when it comes to their SEO campaign.

Regular reporting, reviews and campaign clarity are central to our ethos as an agency. We also love to demonstrate the successes that campaigns have had. Clients relish the results and we enjoy the ego boost too – it’s a win win!
